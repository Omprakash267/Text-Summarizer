The human heart is a muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body. It beats approximately 100,000 times per day. The heart has four chambers: two atria and two ventricles. Oxygen-rich blood is pumped to the body while oxygen-poor blood goes to the lungs.
Artificial intelligence is transforming many industries. Machine learning algorithms can now detect diseases, drive cars, and write text. Deep learning models trained on vast datasets have achieved superhuman performance in several tasks.
Climate change refers to long-term shifts in global temperatures and weather patterns. Human activities since the 1800s have been the main driver of climate change. Burning fossil fuels generates greenhouse gas emissions that trap heat in the atmosphere.
